Company Registration & Incorporation

Complete end-to-end incorporation services for every business structure — Private Limited, LLP, OPC, Section 8, and more — handled by qualified Company Secretaries and Advocates on the MCA portal.

Why Business Structure Matters

Choosing the right legal entity is the single most consequential decision at the start of your business journey. The structure you pick governs your tax liability, the ease of raising institutional funding, the extent of personal liability, and the compliance obligations you must meet for the entire life of the business. An incorrect choice can cost significantly more to rectify later.

At SYNS Associates, our incorporation specialists conduct a thorough needs assessment — covering your sector, investor expectations, workforce size, and long-term vision — before recommending the optimal structure. We then handle every step of the MCA incorporation process, typically completing registration within 10–15 working days of receiving your documents.

Business Structures We Register

🏢

Private Limited Company

The preferred structure for growth-oriented businesses and startups seeking venture capital. Offers limited liability, separate legal identity, and ease of ownership transfer. Governed by Companies Act, 2013.

🤝

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

Combines the flexibility of a partnership with the benefit of limited liability. Ideal for professional service firms, consultancies, and businesses not seeking VC funding. Governed by LLP Act, 2008.

👤

One Person Company (OPC)

Enables a single entrepreneur to enjoy corporate status and limited liability without a co-founder. Mandatory conversion to Pvt Ltd on crossing ₹2 crore paid-up capital or ₹2 crore turnover.

🌐

Public Limited Company

Required for businesses planning a stock exchange listing or public fund-raising. Carries stricter governance requirements including a minimum of 3 directors and 7 shareholders.

💚

Section 8 Company (NGO)

Non-profit companies formed for charitable, educational, scientific, or social objectives. Exempt from the word "Limited" and eligible for various tax exemptions under Income Tax Act.

🏭

Nidhi Company

A specialised NBFC category under the Companies Act, 2013, engaged in borrowing and lending among its members. Requires at least 200 members and ₹10 lakh net-owned funds within one year.

Our Incorporation Process

  1. Business Structure AdvisoryFree 30-minute consultation to identify the optimal entity type based on your objectives, sector, and investor expectations.
  2. Document Collection & Name ApprovalWe collect KYC documents, draft the name application, and check for conflicting trademarks or company names on MCA before filing.
  3. DSC & DIN ProcurementClass 3 DSC is applied for via authorised Certifying Authorities (CAs). DIN is integrated into the main SPICe+ form for companies.
  4. MoA/AoA or Agreement DraftingOur Company Secretaries draft all constitutional documents, customised to your business activity codes (NIC codes) and intended operations.
  5. MCA Filing & ApprovalWe file the complete SPICe+ form (or FiLLiP for LLP) and liaise with the Registrar of Companies for any queries or resubmissions until approval is received.
  6. Post-Incorporation Compliance SetupCoI delivery, share certificate preparation, first board resolution, statutory register setup, and guidance on commencement of business (INC-20A) filing.

Post-Incorporation Compliance You Must Know

  • Every company must file INC-20A (Commencement of Business) within 180 days of incorporation if it has share capital — failure carries ₹50,000 penalty on the company.
  • First Board Meeting must be held within 30 days of incorporation and minutes maintained in a statutory register.
  • Appointment of statutory auditor within 30 days of incorporation (Form ADT-1).
  • Annual compliance: AOC-4, MGT-7, DIR-3 KYC, and income tax returns are mandatory every year from the first financial year, even with zero transactions.

CCFS-2026 Amnesty: The MCA's Company Fresh Start Scheme (CCFS-2026) offers a 90% waiver on accumulated late fees for overdue filings. The window runs 15 April to 15 July 2026 — ideal for companies with pending ROC filings to clear their backlog at minimal cost.

Comprehensive labour law compliance covering PF, ESIC, gratuity, the four new Labour Codes (effective November 2025), Shops & Establishments Act, payroll structuring, and labour audits — protecting your business and your employees.

India's Labour Law Landscape Has Changed

India undertook its most significant labour law reform in decades when it notified all four Labour Codes on 21 November 2025, consolidating 29 separate central labour laws into four unified Codes. While state-level Shops and Establishments Acts and notifications remain operative pending state-level implementation, every employer with a workforce in India is now required to align their HR policies, payroll structure, and benefit calculations with the new Code framework.

The single most impactful change is the 50% Wage Rule — the Code on Wages mandates that basic wages must constitute at least 50% of an employee's total CTC. This structurally increases the PF contribution base, gratuity liability, and leave encashment obligations for most employers by 5–15%. Non-compliance with the new Codes can result in imprisonment of up to 3 years for responsible managers under certain provisions.

Our Labour Law Compliance Services

  • EPF / PF Registration & Compliance: Employer registration with EPFO, monthly ECR (Electronic Challan cum Return) filing, UAN generation for new employees, KYC linking, and annual return filing. Threshold: 20 or more employees (can voluntarily register with fewer).
  • ESIC Registration & Compliance: Employer registration with ESIC, monthly contribution challan filing (employer: 3.25%, employee: 0.75% of wages), and bi-annual returns. Under the new Code on Social Security, ESIC coverage mandates apply from even ONE employee in a hazardous occupation.
  • Gratuity Compliance: Gratuity calculation, trust management or LIC Group Gratuity Scheme advisory, and Form I/J filing. Under the new Code, fixed-term employees are eligible for pro-rata gratuity after just ONE year of service (previously 5 years).
  • Payroll Structuring & 50% Wage Rule Compliance: Restructuring existing CTC to comply with the 50% basic wage rule — computing revised PF, ESIC, gratuity, and bonus obligations and advising on total employer cost impact.
  • Shops & Establishments Act Registration: State-specific registration under the applicable S&E Act (requirements, fees, and processes vary by state). We cover Delhi, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, and other major states.
  • Contract Labour Act Compliance: Principal employer registration with the licensing authority, contractor licence, Register A and B maintenance, Form XII return, and annual returns under the Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970 (now under OSH Code).
  • Minimum Wages Compliance: State-specific minimum wage tracking and payroll verification for all employee categories across notified skill levels (unskilled, semi-skilled, skilled, highly-skilled).
  • Sexual Harassment (POSH) Compliance: Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) formation, POSH policy drafting, annual awareness training, and Annual Report to District Officer.
  • Labour Audit: Comprehensive labour law audit to identify gaps across PF, ESIC, gratuity, S&E Act, minimum wages, and leave records — with a remediation roadmap.
  • Employment Agreements & HR Policy Drafting: Employment contracts, appointment letters, HR manuals, standing orders, and NDA frameworks aligned with the new Code requirements.

ROC & MCA Compliance Filings

Complete annual and event-based ROC compliance for Private Limited Companies, LLPs, and OPCs — MGT-7, AOC-4, Director KYC, DPT-3, and all MCA e-forms filed accurately and on time.

ROC Compliance — A Year-Round Obligation

Every company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, and every LLP registered under the LLP Act, 2008, is required to maintain statutory registers, hold board and general meetings as prescribed, and file annual returns and financial statements with the Registrar of Companies (ROC). These obligations apply from the very first financial year — even if the company has not commenced any business activity or recorded a single transaction.

Failure to file ROC returns on time attracts a late fee of ₹100 per day per form with no cap. After three consecutive years of non-filing, directors face disqualification under Section 164(2) of the Companies Act, 2013, rendering them ineligible to be appointed as a director in any company for 5 years. SYNS Associates manages the entire ROC compliance calendar for our clients, from AGM documentation to event-based filings.

Annual Compliance Services

  • AOC-4 (Financial Statements): Filing of audited financials (Balance Sheet, P&L, Cash Flow Statement) along with the Board's Report and Auditor's Report. Due within 30 days of AGM (typically by 30 October each year for companies with September 30 AGM).
  • MGT-7 / MGT-7A (Annual Return): Annual return disclosing shareholders, directors, share capital, and debentures. Due within 60 days of AGM (typically 29 November). MGT-7A is filed by OPCs and small companies.
  • ADT-1 (Auditor Appointment): Filing within 15 days of AGM confirming appointment or re-appointment of the statutory auditor for each block of 5 years.
  • DIR-3 KYC (Director KYC): The MCA shifted DIR-3 KYC from annual to triennial filing from FY 2025–26 onwards. Directors who completed KYC before FY 2025–26 will next file by 30 June 2028. New DIN holders must file within 30 days of allotment.
  • Annual General Meeting (AGM) Compliance: Notice drafting, statutory agenda preparation, proxy forms, minutes of AGM, and post-AGM statutory filings.
  • Board Meeting Documentation: Notice, agenda, and minutes for all board meetings (minimum 4 per year for companies; first meeting within 30 days of incorporation).

Event-Based MCA Filings

  • MGT-14: Filing of resolutions passed by the Board or shareholders (within 30 days) — including changes to business objects, alteration of MoA/AoA, approval of related party transactions.
  • SH-7 (Increase in Authorised Share Capital): Filed within 30 days of passing the special resolution.
  • PAS-3 (Return of Allotment): Filed within 30 days of allotment of shares (equity, preference, or convertible instruments).
  • SH-4 (Share Transfer): Form for transfer of physical shares executed within 60 days of date of instrument.
  • DIR-12 (Change in Directors): Appointment, resignation, or vacation of office of directors — filed within 30 days.
  • INC-22 (Registered Office Change): Change of registered office within or outside state — filed within 15/30 days as applicable.
  • DPT-3 (Return of Deposits): Annual return of deposits and outstanding loans/borrowings from members. Due by 30 June each year.
  • MSME Form 1: Half-yearly return disclosing outstanding payments to MSME suppliers. Due 31 October and 30 April.
  • INC-20A (Commencement of Business): Filed within 180 days of incorporation for companies with share capital — certifying that paid-up capital has been received in the company's bank account.

LLP Annual Compliance

  • Form 11 (LLP Annual Return): Details of partners and contributions — due by 30 May each year.
  • Form 8 (LLP Statement of Accounts): Statement of accounts and solvency — due by 30 October each year.
  • LLP-3 (LLP Agreement / Supplementary Agreement): Filed within 30 days of execution of LLP agreement or any supplementary agreement.

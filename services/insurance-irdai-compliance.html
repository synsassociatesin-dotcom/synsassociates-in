Insurance Company Compliance

End-to-end IRDAI compliance for life, general, and health insurers — from R1/R2/R3 licensing and regulatory filings to solvency management, compliance framework implementation, internal governance structuring, and continuous regulatory monitoring support.

The IRDAI Regulatory Framework

The Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) is the apex regulator governing all insurance business in India under the Insurance Act, 1938 and the IRDAI Act, 1999. Every insurer — whether life, general, or health — must obtain a Certificate of Registration (CoR) from IRDAI before commencing operations, and thereafter comply with an extensive and continuously evolving calendar of regulatory obligations.

Non-compliance attracts penalties of up to ₹1 crore per violation and ₹1 lakh per day for continuing defaults, in addition to the risk of licence suspension. SYNS Associates provides comprehensive end-to-end IRDAI compliance support, enabling insurers and insurance intermediaries to focus on business while we manage the regulatory burden.

Our Insurance Compliance Services

  • Licensing & Regulatory Filings: We assist in obtaining IRDAI registration through the R1, R2, and R3 application process for life, general, health, and reinsurance companies, as well as licence approvals for intermediaries including insurance agents, brokers, corporate agents, web aggregators, and TPAs. We also manage the annual, quarterly, and monthly statutory report submissions to the IRDAI authority.
  • Regulatory Advisory: Our legal team provides comprehensive advisory on all matters governed by IRDAI, the Insurance Act 1938, the Companies Act 2013, and applicable FEMA provisions. We actively interpret newly issued IRDAI circulars and master guidelines and translate their requirements into actionable operational changes for your organisation.
  • Compliance Framework Implementation: We assist insurers in building a robust compliance framework fully aligned with IRDAI's governance rules, solvency guidelines, and reporting requirements. This includes designing internal compliance policies, establishing escalation structures, and integrating regulatory requirements into day-to-day business processes.
  • Solvency & Risk Management: Our finance and actuarial advisory covers solvency margin computation and monitoring (minimum 150% ratio), actuarial valuation report coordination, financial disclosures, regulatory audit preparation, and support for underwriting and operational risk management. We assist with solvency and investment compliance under the IRDAI (Actuarial, Finance & Investment) Regulations 2024.
  • Internal Corporate Structuring: We help insurance entities establish board committees, governance structures, and internal controls mandated by IRDAI. Our team drafts risk management frameworks, whistleblower policies, outsourcing policies, and internal audit charters. We also assist with "Fit & Proper" declarations for directors, key management persons, and promoters.
  • Compliance Documentation: We develop the full suite of compliance documentation — internal policies, SOPs, and regulatory submissions. This includes setting up a policyholder grievance redressal framework, drafting policyholder-facing guidelines, and maintaining all statutory registers, records, and compliance paperwork to ensure audit readiness at all times.
  • Monitoring & Inspection Support: We conduct gap assessments against prevailing IRDAI regulations to identify compliance shortfalls before they attract regulatory attention. Where gaps are found, we provide corrective action plans and full implementation support. Our team coordinates directly with IRDAI during inspections, providing responses, clarifications, and compliance certificates on your behalf.
  • IT & Cybersecurity Compliance: We assist insurers in complying with the IRDAI Information and Cyber Security Guidelines 2023 — drafting IT data protection policies, incident response frameworks, and facilitating security audits including VAPT. We also advise on regulatory requirements for digital insurance distribution and online sales under applicable IRDAI guidelines.
  • Operational & Policyholder Compliance: We support product filing and approval submissions with IRDAI, ensure claims processing procedures comply with regulations, and assist with underwriting compliance. Our team helps implement consumer protection and policyholder grievance redressal frameworks and conducts due diligence on compliance obligations specific to your product lines.
  • Continuous Ongoing Monitoring: Our legal researchers track regulatory updates, compliance amendments, and IRDAI calendar changes on an ongoing basis and keep your organisation informed and prepared. We provide timely advisory on required operational and governance adjustments, ensuring your business remains aligned with IRDAI's evolving regulatory expectations throughout the year.

IRDAI Penalty Framework: Non-compliance with IRDAI regulations attracts monetary penalties up to ₹1 crore per violation under Section 102 of the Insurance Act, 1938, with an additional ₹1 lakh per day for continuing defaults. Serious violations can result in suspension or cancellation of the Certificate of Registration. Proactive compliance is always more cost-effective than remediation after regulatory action.

Corporate Law & Advisory

Corporate Legal Services

End-to-end corporate legal advisory for businesses at every stage — mergers & acquisitions, joint ventures, shareholders' agreements, board governance, corporate restructuring, legal due diligence, and investment documentation across all sectors.

What Corporate Legal Services Cover

Corporate legal services encompass the full range of legal advisory and documentation needs that arise in the life of a business — from incorporation through growth, fundraising, strategic transactions, and restructuring. Unlike general legal consultancy, corporate legal work is transactional and governance-focused: it is concerned with how businesses are structured, how agreements between stakeholders are documented, and how regulatory obligations arising from corporate actions are met.

At SYNS Associates, our corporate legal team advises founders, promoters, boards, investors, and institutions on matters ranging from simple shareholder agreements to complex cross-border M&A transactions and NCLT-driven restructuring schemes. We combine legal precision with commercial practicality — ensuring that every document we draft or transaction we advise on is both legally sound and business-ready.

Our Corporate Legal Services

  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Transaction structuring for share purchases, asset purchases, and business transfers. End-to-end advisory covering legal due diligence, SPA and SHA drafting, regulatory approvals (CCI, NCLT, RBI/FEMA where applicable), conditions precedent management, and post-merger integration compliance under the Companies Act, 2013.
  • Joint Ventures & Collaborations: Structuring domestic and cross-border joint ventures, drafting JV agreements and partnership deeds, advising on foreign collaboration approvals under FEMA, and building in clear exit mechanisms — put options, buy-back rights, and tag-along provisions.
  • Shareholders' Agreements (SHA): Drafting and negotiating comprehensive SHAs covering governance rights, founder vesting schedules, tag-along and drag-along rights, anti-dilution clauses (broad-based weighted average, full ratchet), information rights, reserved matters, and dispute resolution mechanisms.
  • Board Governance & Secretarial Advisory: Advising boards on statutory obligations under the Companies Act, 2013 — board meeting procedures, committee charters, statutory registers, director disclosure requirements, related-party transaction approvals, and secretarial compliance for private and public companies.
  • Corporate Restructuring: Advisory and documentation for mergers, demergers, amalgamations, slump sales, business transfers, capital reduction, and buy-back of shares. Preparation and filing of schemes with NCLT, coordination with auditors and valuers, and post-order compliance filings with the Registrar of Companies.
  • Legal Due Diligence: Comprehensive corporate, title, regulatory, and litigation due diligence for acquisitions, private equity investments, and strategic partnerships. We deliver structured due diligence reports with risk ratings and recommended conditions to be addressed before transaction closure.
  • Legal Opinions & Advisory: Written legal opinions on corporate, regulatory, contractual, and transactional matters — sought by businesses, investors, lenders, and foreign entities entering India. Opinions are backed by thorough research and clearly state the basis, assumptions, and qualifications.
  • Investment Documentation: Drafting and reviewing term sheets, subscription and shareholder agreements, convertible notes, SAFE agreements, compulsorily convertible debenture (CCD) documents, investor rights agreements, and conditions precedent checklists for seed, Series A, and later-stage funding rounds.

Why Corporate Legal Advice Matters

Proceeding without proper legal structuring in corporate transactions exposes businesses and their founders to significant risk — disputes between shareholders with no clear resolution mechanism, undisclosed liabilities surfacing post-acquisition, regulatory penalties for non-compliance with FEMA or Companies Act requirements, and failed transactions due to defective documentation. The cost of legal advice upfront is a fraction of the cost of litigation or regulatory action after the fact. Well-drafted agreements also provide clarity and confidence to all parties, reducing the likelihood of disputes and enabling faster decision-making as the business grows.

Note on Cross-Border Transactions: M&A and joint venture transactions involving foreign investors or overseas entities require compliance with FEMA regulations, RBI approvals where applicable, and CCI merger control filings above applicable thresholds. SYNS Associates provides integrated advisory covering both the corporate law and regulatory dimensions of cross-border deals, ensuring no compliance step is missed.

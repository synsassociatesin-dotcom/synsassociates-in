We're Hiring

Build Your Career in Compliance & Legal Advisory

Join a growing team of Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, and legal professionals helping India's businesses stay compliant and competitive. We offer real responsibility from day one and a work environment built on mentorship, integrity, and continuous learning.

Why Build Your Career at SYNS Associates?

We are a specialist compliance and legal advisory firm — not a generalist firm where you get lost in the crowd. Every team member works directly on substantive client matters across RBI, SEBI, MCA, income tax, GST, and labour law. You will not spend years doing routine audit support — you will handle real regulatory matters from the start.

📚

Steep Learning Curve

Exposure to complex, multi-regulator matters — FEMA + GST + Companies Act intersections — that you simply would not encounter at a single-focus practice. Regulatory change briefings every month keep you ahead of the law.

🏢

Diverse Client Base

Work with startups raising angel rounds, mid-market companies navigating SEBI LODR, NBFCs restructuring under the new 2025 RBI framework, and exporters dealing with FEMA compounding. Every week brings a genuinely new problem.

🎯

Meritocracy Over Seniority

Promotions and client responsibility are driven by output, not years of service. A sharp associate who delivers quality work earns client-facing responsibility faster here than at firms where hierarchy determines opportunity.

🤝

Direct Mentorship

You work directly alongside senior practitioners — not through layers of supervision. Partners and senior associates review your work and explain the reasoning, not just the corrections. We invest in building your judgement, not just your task list.

⚖️

Work-Life Integrity

Compliance work has natural rhythms around filing deadlines, not manufactured crises. We plan capacity carefully so that deadline months are manageable, not chaotic. Our Monday–Saturday, 9:30 AM–6:30 PM schedule is real, not aspirational.

🚀

Growing Practice Areas

SYNS Associates is expanding across SEBI compliance, startup advisory, and FEMA/ODI practice — growth areas driven by regulatory change in India. Joining now means growing with the firm into practice areas that will define the next decade.

Our Hiring Process

We keep our process short and transparent. We respect your time and aim to complete the process within two weeks of your application.

1
Apply Online
Send your CV and a brief cover note (3–5 sentences on why this role) to our contact form or directly to our email. No elaborate application needed.
2
Screening Call
A 20-minute call with a senior associate to understand your background, discuss the role, and answer your questions. We assess fit in both directions.
3
Technical Round
A practical, take-home assignment relevant to the role — a short drafting task, a compliance computation, or a research memo. Typically 1–2 hours. No trick questions.
4
Partner Interview
A 45-minute conversation with the founding partner. We discuss the assignment, your approach to complex problems, and our expectations. Offer letter issued within 3 business days.

What We Look For in Every Candidate

Beyond technical qualifications, we look for qualities that determine whether someone thrives in a specialist advisory environment.

Intellectual honesty: We value candidates who say "I don't know, but I will find out" over those who confabulate an answer. Compliance work requires precision — we need people who flag uncertainty rather than paper over it.

  • Regulatory curiosity — you read the Finance Act or MCA circular out of interest, not just duty. India's regulatory landscape changes fast; people who stay current organically are far more valuable than those who wait to be briefed.
  • Client communication — the ability to translate a complex regulatory provision into a plain-language email that a business owner can act on. This is a skill we develop, but we need baseline written clarity.
  • Deadline discipline — compliance is deadline-driven. We need professionals who plan backwards from filing dates, flag risks early, and never miss a statutory deadline because of poor planning.
  • Ownership mentality — we are a focused team. Everyone owns their client files end-to-end. We do not have a culture of escalating routine decisions or waiting for instructions on standard matters.
